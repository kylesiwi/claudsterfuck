# Socratic Probing

Probe for:

- unclear terms
- hidden assumptions
- weak evidence
- missing perspectives
- downstream consequences
- wrong problem framing

Useful prompts:

- What assumption does this depend on?
- What happens if that assumption is false?
- What evidence supports this choice here?
- How does this look from on-call, security, or support?
- What becomes harder to change after this ships?
- Are we solving the real problem or an attempted workaround?
